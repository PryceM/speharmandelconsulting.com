The SMC Method

Map. Diagnose. Design. Execute. Transfer.

Map

Understand people, process, tools, money, customers, incentives, constraints, and decision points.

Diagnose

Identify the real block: strategy, operations, technology, capital logic, or the space between them.

Design

Create a practical plan that fits the organization’s size, resources, timeline, and tolerance for change.

Execute

Move the work forward through materials, workflows, research, training, implementation, and decision support.

Transfer

Leave behind a system the team can understand, use, and maintain.

How we think

Patterns we solve.

A funding story is not a pitch deck problem.

It is a logic problem: what is being built, why now, why this team, why this capital, and why this return.

Technology does not fail because teams hate software.

It fails because nobody mapped the workflow before buying the tool.

Acquisitions fail after the close.

The question is not just price. It is whether the operating system can absorb the decision.
